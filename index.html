What we do

We enable software teams to design and build better software by:

  • Upskilling them in current software development methodologies and best practices.
  • Strengthening their ability to plan/manage work and communicate effectively with stakeholders.

Services

Apprentice programs

For less-experienced developers. Kohais (apprentices) learn from our Senpais through classroom sessions, projects and personal mentoring.

Advanced programs

Train experienced developers in areas such as Agile, Behaviordriven development, Continuous delivery, DevOps, Event storming etc.

In-company programs

Blend training and coaching with consulting to address specific needs of IT teams as well as software product owners / managers

Our Values

Our passion to help your teams build better software is supported by our organizational values. Collectively, these values help us deliver value to every customer organization and every individual we work with, besides guiding our own evolution as a business.

Mutual respect and caring

We respect each other and all our customers; empathy and care contribute to creating a safe, enjoyable and productive workplace.

Excellence

We strive to deliver the best solution for our customers at all times..

Kaizen

We actively seek to improve our knowledge and ways of working to add value to all stakeholders.

Autonomy

We encourage “self-management” rooted in trust and integrity in the pursuit of personal and professional excellence.

Knowledge sharing

We share our knowledge to help each other and our customers.

Sustainability

We are committed to diversity & inclusion, encouraging a healthy work-life balance and environmental consciousness.
